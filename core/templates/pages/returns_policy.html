Returns & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we're here to help.

1. Return & Exchange Window

You can return or exchange most items purchased from Present Pickz within 7 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition, unused, and with all tags and packaging intact.

2. Eligible for Returns

Items can be returned if:

  • The product is damaged or defective upon arrival.
  • The product received is different from what was ordered.
  • The product has missing parts or accessories.
  • You have changed your mind (subject to item category exceptions).

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Gift cards
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Opened or used beauty/personal care products

4. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

  • Approved Refunds: The refund will be processed and automatically credited to your original method of payment within 5-7 business days.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) Orders: For COD orders, the refund will be processed as Present Pickz store credit or via bank transfer to an account provided by you.

5. Shipping Costs for Returns

If the return is due to our error (defective or wrong item), we will bear the return shipping costs. For returns due to change of mind, the customer is responsible for return shipping arrangements and costs.

6. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, you can:

  1. Go to My Orders in your account.
  2. Select the order and click on Return/Exchange.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to provide a reason and photos (if required).
  4. Alternatively, contact us at support@presentpickz.com.
