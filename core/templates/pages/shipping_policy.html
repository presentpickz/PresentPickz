1. Delivery Timelines

We strive to deliver your orders as quickly as possible. Typical delivery times are:

  • Metro Cities: 2-3 business days.
  • Other Cities: 4-6 business days.
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 business days.

Please note that these are estimated delivery times and actual delivery might vary depending on the destination and courier availability.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on the weight of the items and the delivery location. You can view the exact shipping cost during the checkout process.

  • Free Shipping: Orders above ₹999 are eligible for free standard shipping.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email and SMS with the tracking details. You can also track your order directly from the Track Order page on our website using your Order ID.

4. Shipping Partners

We partner with reliable logistics companies like BlueDart, Delhivery, and Ecom Express to ensure your packages are handled with care and delivered on time.

5. Packing & Handling

All items are packed securely to prevent any damage during transit. We also offer premium gift wrapping for a small additional charge, which can be selected at the time of checkout.

6. Damaged Shipments

If you receive a package that is visibly damaged or tampered with, please do not accept the delivery and contact our customer support immediately at +91 8778992588.
