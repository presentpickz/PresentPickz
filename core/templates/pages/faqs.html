Shipping & Delivery

We typically process and ship orders within 24-48 hours. Once shipped, delivery usually takes:

  • Metro Cities: 2-4 business days
  • Rest of India: 4-7 business days

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS and Email with the tracking link. You can also track your order status in real-time from the My Orders section in your account.

We offer Free Shipping on all prepaid orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999 or Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, a nominal shipping fee of ₹40-₹60 may apply depending on your location.

Returns & Refunds

We have a 7-day easy return policy. You can return products if they are damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered. The item must be unused and in its original packaging with tags intact.

Refunds are processed within 24-48 hours after we receive the returned item. The amount will be credited back to your original payment mode within 5-7 business days.

Orders & Payments

We accept all major payment methods including:

  • UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
  • Net Banking
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
